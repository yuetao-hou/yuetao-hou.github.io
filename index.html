Yuetao Hou

I am Yuetao Hou, a PhD student in ECE at Cornell University working at High Frequency Power Electronics Lab. I work on high frequency power electronics.

I interned at Kilby Labs of Texas Instruments in summer 2022 as a System Engineer Intern where I worked on active EMI filters.

Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree at Xian Jiaotong University, where I worked with Prof. Wenjie Chen and Prof. Xu Yang on wireless power transfer. I was a member of Special Class for the Gifted Young. I visited The Center for High Performance Power Electronics as a visiting student intern advised by Prof. Jin Wang.

Research

I work on circuits, specifically power electronics circuit. Most recently, I am particularly interested in high frequency power electronics and its emerging applications in fusion.
